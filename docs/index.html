Multilingual Speech Testing on GitHub

Speech Inteligibility Test

We present a collection of real-world speech recordings in several languages under CC-BY-SA-4.0 license.

The datasets are designed for speech quality and intelligibility assessment purposes, including subjective tests in laboratory or crowdsourced settings. We also provide a survey setup tool to create your own survey.
